Planes

RC Combat planes flown by KCF are 1/12 size WWII scale aircraft. Wingspans range from 38" to 41" with the airframes being made from coroplast, balsa, fiberglass or some combination of those three materials. Coroplast is the most common material used for combat by KCF due to it's durability and abundant supply.

Combat planes are powered by glow engines that burn nitromethene (5%-25%) for fuel.
