About Kawainui Model Air Field

Kawainui Model Airplane field is located within within Kawainui Marsh just south of Kailua town on the Hawaiian island of Oahu. The park itself is managed by the City & County of Honolulu. The field is approximately 5.5 acres and is nested within the 1,000 acre Kawainui Wetland Preserve. It is open from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM 365 days a year to all members of the public.

The park offers gender dedicated bathrooms, water spigot with a hose and a paved (albeit) uneven parking lot.

The field allows for fixed wing flight on the west side of the park and for rotary power on the east side. On Sundays the entire field is reserved for glider flying until 1:00 PM.
Flight OPs for RC combat flying occur on Saturdays between 12:00 - 5:00 PM. The field may also be reserved for events via permit obtained through the Department of Parks and Recreation.

Prevailing winds out of the north promote a left hand pattern of flying with landing approached being made over Kapaa Quarry Road.
