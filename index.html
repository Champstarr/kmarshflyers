    • Kawainui Combat Flyers (KCF) promotes the recreational hobby/sport of radio controlled model aircraft flying. KCF primairly flies 1/12 scale combat R/C aircraft at the Kawainui Flying Field in Kailua, Hawaii on the island of Oahu.

Status of Kawainui Model Airfield

Kawainui Model Airfield has resumed normal hours and is open from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM Monday - Friday. Sunday's from 7:00 AM to 1:00 PM is reserved for glider flying.

Kawainui COVID-19 Mandates

Permits are required for all shade canopies at Kawainui Model Airfield. Permits can be instantly obtained free of charge here at the Honolulu County Parks County website


Masks are required at the field per County of Honolulu mandate.
